Our Heritage

Where Earth's
Finest Gems
Find Their Home

Nestled in the heart of historic Galle, The Gem World Holdings is a premier destination for those who appreciate the rare, the radiant, and the refined. We specialise in Sri Lanka's world-renowned gemstones — sapphires, rubies, cat's eye, alexandrite, and more.

Every gem in our collection is hand-selected by our expert gemmologists, certified for authenticity, and presented with the transparency you deserve. Whether you seek a treasured keepsake or an investment-grade stone, we guide you with knowledge and passion.
